MUSTANG

Mustang, often called the “Last Forbidden Kingdom,” is a mystical land nestled in the Himalayas of Nepal. Known for its dramatic desert landscapes, ancient caves.

TAJMAHAL

The Taj Mahal, located in Agra, India, is one of the most iconic monuments in the world. Built by Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his beloved wife Mumtaz Mahal.

GOA

Goa is India’s most lively beach destination, famous for its golden sand beaches, vibrant nightlife, calm blue waters, and rich Portuguese-influenced culture.

Manali

Manali is a peaceful hill town in the Himalayas known for its snow-capped mountains, pine forests, and fresh mountain air. Whether it’s relaxing beside the Beas River.

Kedarnath

Kedarnath is one of India’s most revered spiritual destinations, located high in the Himalayas of Uttarakhand. Surrounded by snow-covered peaks and serene valleys.
